Exercise

Exercise

Exercise is movement for the body and mind. Exercise is important to keep our heart and bodies healthy.

Typically, I exercise 5 days a week 1 hour a day. I focus on 30 minutes of cardio and 20 minutes of strength training. I find that moving my body helps me focus and gives me energy to be the best wife, mother, teacher that I can be.

Cooking

Cooking

Cooking is good for the soul. Not matter what you cook in the kitchen it slows you down and helps you process what you are doing. I enjoy cooking for my family and turning on my amazon Alexa and listening to music. I cook a variety of different meals for my family. Specifically mexican style, chicken pasta meals, baseball nights crock pot meals, pot roast, sunday dinner is one of my favorite meals to cook. I recently started baking cakes. My favorite two cakes to make are blueberry with creamcheese icing and keylime cake. A fun fact about me. I clean my kitchen while I cook so I don't have a pile of dirty dishes at the end.
